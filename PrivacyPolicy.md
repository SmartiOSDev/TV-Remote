## 3. Local Network Access

### 3.1 Why We Need Network Access
The Roku Remote app requires access to your local network to:
- Discover Roku devices using SSDP (Simple Service Discovery Protocol)
- Send commands to your Roku device via the External Control Protocol (ECP)
- Maintain connection with your Roku

### 3.2 Network Security
- All communication between the app and your Roku device happens locally
- No Roku control data is sent to external servers
- Your Roku commands are sent directly from your iPhone to your Roku

### 3.3 Network Privacy Statement
**Your network data stays on your network.** The app does not transmit your Roku device information, IP addresses, or control commands to any external servers.
